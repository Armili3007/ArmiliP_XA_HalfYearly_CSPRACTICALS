Welcome to Nehru Zoological Park

Explore the Wonders of Wildlife!

Nestled in the heart of our vibrant city, Nehru Zoological Park spans 380 acres of lush greenery and captivating animal exhibits. Home to a diverse range of species, our park invites visitors of all ages to discover the beauty of nature and the importance of wildlife conservation. Come and immerse yourself in an unforgettable experience that inspires appreciation and respect for the animal kingdom!
