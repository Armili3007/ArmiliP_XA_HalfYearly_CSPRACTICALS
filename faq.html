Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Q1) What are the operating hours of the Animal Park?

A) 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM on Weekdays. 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM on Weekends.

Q2) Are outside food and beverages allowed?

A) Yes. You are welcome to bring outside food and beverages.

Q3) Do you offer guided tours?

A) Yes, we offer guided tours at the animal park. They provide insights into our wildlife and conservation efforts.

Q4) Is there a parking facility available?

A) Yes, there is a parking facility available at the animal park for visitors. It offers convenient parking options to ensure a hassle-free experience.

Q5) Can we bring pets to the National Park?

A) No, pets are not allowed in the National Park to ensure the safety of both animals and visitors.
